What are the benefits of AI in real estate?

AI can help real estate agents boost productivity, efficiency, and accuracy, while also providing data-driven insights and personalized recommendations.

Is AI a threat to real estate agents?

No, AI is designed to assist real estate agents, not replace them. It can help agents focus on high-level tasks and provide better results for their clients.
